The Thesaurus Musicarum Latinarum Revivificatus (TMLr) is an adaptation of a database of Latin-language texts about music theory. The original website, a project of Indiana University Jacobs School of Music's Center for the History of Music Theory and Literature, started in the 1990s and digitized hundreds of texts, but is sadly defunct. The TMLr thus aims to preserve this important copus of texts for the broader scholarly community.
